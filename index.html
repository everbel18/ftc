Old City Free Walking Tour

We will visit the main points of interest in Cartagena's walled city (Corralito de Piedra), so you can discover and enjoy the history of Cartagena…

This is a scenic walking tour where, instead of visiting museums, we immerse ourselves in the essence of the city. We start at Plaza Santa Teresa with an introduction to the history of Cartagena, to then walk through iconic places such as Plaza San Pedro Claver, Plaza de la Aduana, the legendary Torre del Reloj, and Plaza de la Proclamación. We will also visit the Catedral de Santa Catalina, Parque Simón Bolívar, and the iconic plazas of Santo Domingo and de la Merced, discovering the secrets held within every corner of the Walled City.

  • Meeting Point: Plaza Santa Teresa
  • Departures: Every day
  • Schedule: 10:00 a.m.
  • Language: English/Spanish
  • Duration: 2 hours

Bazurto Market
Food Tour

Taste local delights: arepas, empanadas, ceviche, and traditional sweets while walking through the city.

Bazurto Market is a traditional market offering fish, meat, fruit, and clothing, and it is as significant in Cartagena as the Old City or the 'little Miami' (Bocagrande). It is a very traditional market, barely visited by tourists.

We will walk through a labyrinth of narrow streets, where the tour guide will introduce you to all kinds of fruits, vegetables, fish, seafood, and meats. We will taste different fruits and enjoy a delicious, typical lunch.

At the end of our visit to Bazurto Market, you will enjoy a cold Costeñita beer in a popular bar.

  • Meeting Point: Plaza Santa Teresa
  • Departures: Monday to Saturday
  • Time: 9:00 a.m.
  • Language: Spanish/English
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Price: 50 USD per person (Minimum 2 people)

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tours in Cartagena.

Why choose Free Tour Cartagena?

We are the top-rated choice for travelers. We are pioneers since 2014, and for 10 consecutive years, we have received the TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence. More than 100,000 tourists have trusted us.

Where does the Free Tour Cartagena start?

The meeting point is at Plaza Santa Teresa, right outside the Naval Museum. Look for our guides with yellow umbrellas.

Are the tours available in English?

Yes! We provide specialized professional guides for both English and Spanish speakers every day.

Do you only offer free walking tours?

No! We also offer specialized experiences like our famous Street Food Tour, Private City Tours, and excursions to San Basilio de Palenque.

Is it safe to walk in Cartagena?

Yes, the Historic Center and Getsemaní are very safe for walking. However, joining a guided tour is the best way to enjoy the city with total peace of mind and local expertise.

Which is better: Old City or Getsemaní?

Both are must-sees! The Walled City offers history and colonial architecture, while Getsemaní offers vibrant street art and local vibes. Our tours cover both to give you the full experience.

What should I wear for a walking tour?

Cartagena is hot and humid! Wear light, breathable clothing, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, and a hat. Our morning tours are best for avoiding the midday sun.

Can I use USD or credit cards?

Yes! For your convenience, we accept US Dollars (USD) and Credit Cards for your gratuities or specialized tours. However, we still recommend carrying some Colombian Pesos (COP) for small street purchases.
